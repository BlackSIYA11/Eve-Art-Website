About Me

EA
I am Evelyn, but you can call me Eve. I am an artist with a dream of becoming a 2D animator one day. I am based in Johannesburg, South Africa. My work includes illustrations, graphic design, cartoons, traditional art, character design, 2D animation, glass paintings, and even customized clothing. The list is endless, as I love exploring different art forms and expressing myself creatively. Creative thinking comes naturally to me. I enjoy challenging myself as it helps me grow my skills and refine the ones I already have.

I am open to discussing your ideas and helping you bring them to life! :)
